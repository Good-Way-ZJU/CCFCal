CCFCal Release

CCFCal v1.0.0

A macOS menu bar calendar for tracking the CCF deadlines you actually subscribe to.

Highlights

  • Browse CCF venues by rank, domain, and keyword.
  • Subscribe to selected conferences or journals.
  • Highlight subscribed DDL dates in the calendar.
  • Show the nearest DDL countdown directly in the menu bar.

Data

  • Candidate data is generated from ccfddl-style sources.
  • Installed apps refresh the hosted candidate snapshot at most once per day on launch.
  • Sparkle automatic updates are not enabled in this release.

Install

  • Download and unzip CCFCal.
  • Move CCFCal.app to Applications.
  • Grant Calendar access when prompted.
